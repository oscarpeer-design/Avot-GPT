# Avot-GPT
Experience the timeless wisdom of Perkai Avot for a new age.

The conception of this project came unto me from the son of a community elder. At the time of initiation, he was the most astute 13 year old I had ever met, and had already developed a similar AI system for analysis of religious texts. There were many religious texts I could have chosen for such a project, but I decided upon analysis of Perkai Avot in particular, because it is such a wonderfully concise silo of moral wisdom, with insights that relate to people of all faiths.

I licensed this project with an MIT License in the belief that the fruits of spiritual learning are the boon of all humankind, and that the struggle for moral excellence should absorb all.

Life is a gift. What you do with it matters.
